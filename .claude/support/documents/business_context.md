# Business Context - OEMMatInsightBI

## Project Overview

**Project Name:** OEMMatInsightBI (OEM Material Insight Business Intelligence)

**Domain:** Supply Chain Sustainability Analytics

**Project Type:** Personal Portfolio Project (Microsoft Fabric & Power BI)

## Business Problem

OEM manufacturers need visibility into their supply chain's environmental and governance performance to:

- Assess supplier sustainability credentials (Environmental Performance Index - EPI)
- Evaluate governance quality of supplier countries (World Governance Indicators - WGI)
- Analyze material sourcing patterns and supplier concentration risks
- Track procurement spending against sustainability metrics
- Comply with ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) reporting requirements
- Make data-driven sourcing decisions

## Stakeholders

### Primary Users
- **Procurement Teams:** Analyze spending, supplier performance, and material sourcing
- **Sustainability/ESG Reporting Teams:** Track environmental and governance metrics
- **Supply Chain Risk Management:** Identify concentration risks and supply vulnerabilities

### Secondary Users
- **Executive Leadership:** Dashboard consumers for high-level insights
- **Finance Teams:** Procurement spend analysis
- **Regulatory/Compliance:** ESG reporting requirements

## Success Metrics

### Project Success
- ✅ Complete visibility into supplier environmental performance
- ✅ Ability to identify high-risk sourcing regions
- ✅ Automated ESG reporting from procurement data
- ✅ Power BI dashboards accessible to stakeholders
- ✅ Portfolio-quality demonstration of technical skills

### Business KPIs (if deployed to production)
- % of procurement spend from high-EPI countries (target: >70%)
- Supplier concentration risk index (target: <50% from single country for critical materials)
- Time to generate ESG report (target: <1 day)
- Dashboard adoption rate among stakeholders

## Business Questions Answered

### Procurement Analysis
1. What is our total spend by material/supplier/region?
2. How has spending changed over time (YoY, MoM)?
3. What is our supplier concentration by material?
4. Which materials have the highest unit cost volatility?

### Sustainability Performance
5. What is the average environmental performance of our suppliers?
6. What percentage of spend goes to high/medium/low EPI countries?
7. How do supplier HQ countries compare to production countries in governance?
8. Which materials are sourced from high-risk regions?

### Supply Chain Risk
9. What is our exposure to concentrated supply chains?
10. Which critical materials have >50% supply from one country?
11. How does our procurement align with global supply patterns?

## Business Value Proposition

**For Procurement:**
- Identify cost optimization opportunities
- Negotiate better with concentrated suppliers
- Diversify sourcing to reduce risk

**For Sustainability:**
- Demonstrate ESG compliance
- Prioritize engagement with low-performing suppliers
- Track progress toward sustainability goals

**For Leadership:**
- Data-driven decision making
- Risk mitigation in supply chain
- Competitive advantage through responsible sourcing

## Competitive Landscape

**Similar Solutions:**
- SAP Ariba (Procurement + Sustainability)
- Coupa Supply Chain Design (Risk Management)
- EcoVadis (Supplier Sustainability Ratings)
- Sourcemap (Supply Chain Transparency)

**Differentiation:**
- Integrates public data (EPI, WGI) with procurement data
- Open-source approach (could be adapted by other organizations)
- Focused on critical materials and supply concentration
- Built on modern cloud platform (Microsoft Fabric)

## Constraints & Assumptions

### Constraints
- Single developer (personal project)
- Limited to publicly available sustainability data
- No real-time data (batch processing)
- No procurement system integration (synthetic data)

### Assumptions
- EPI and WGI scores are representative of supplier sustainability
- Country-level metrics apply to all suppliers in that country
- Historical data is sufficient (no predictive analytics needed initially)
- Stakeholders have Power BI access and basic BI literacy

## Future Vision (Post-Portfolio)

**Phase 2 Enhancements:**
- Real-time data integration with procurement systems
- Predictive analytics (forecast spend, identify emerging risks)
- Supplier scorecards with composite sustainability metrics
- Automated alerts for high-risk procurements
- Mobile app for executive dashboards

**Potential Deployment:**
- Adapt for real OEM manufacturing company
- Expand to other industries (electronics, automotive, aerospace)
- Offer as SaaS product for SMB manufacturers

## Glossary

- **OEM:** Original Equipment Manufacturer
- **EPI:** Environmental Performance Index (Yale University)
- **WGI:** World Governance Indicators (World Bank)
- **ESG:** Environmental, Social, and Governance
- **CRM:** Critical Raw Materials (EU classification)
- **Supply Concentration:** % of global supply from single country/source
